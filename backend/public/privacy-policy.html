1. Introduction

Welcome to Instagram DM Commerce ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Instagram direct messaging commerce platform.

Our Commitment: We will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your data is used solely to provide and improve our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, company name, and password
  • Instagram Business Account: Instagram username, account ID, and access tokens
  • Product Information: Product names, descriptions, prices, and images you upload

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Instagram Messages: Direct messages sent to your Instagram business account
  • User Interactions: Message content, timestamps, and user responses
  • Usage Data: Analytics data, click rates, conversion rates, and engagement metrics
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and access times

2.3 Instagram Platform Data

When you connect your Instagram account, we receive:

  • Instagram account profile information
  • Direct messages sent to your account
  • Message delivery and read receipts
  • User engagement data (clicks, impressions)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To process and respond to Instagram direct messages with product recommendations
  • Product Management: To display and manage your product catalog
  • Analytics: To provide insights on message engagement, conversion rates, and performance
  • Communication: To send you service updates, notifications, and support messages
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We interact with Instagram's API to send and receive messages. Data shared with Instagram is subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5.1 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)

  • Regular security audits and updates

  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

Your data is stored securely in cloud infrastructure. We use industry-standard hosting providers with robust security measures.
